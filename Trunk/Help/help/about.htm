Nevod AntiSpam

Overview

The anti spam software takes just one minute to set-up and requires no configuration or updates. Our anti spam software works with any e-mail client that reads its mail via the Internet standard POP3 email protocol including Outlook, Outlook Express, Eudora and Thunderbird.

Nevod AntiSpam is a powerful anti-spam filter combining ease-of-use and a high degree of protection against unsolicited emails.

Nevod AntiSpam runs in the background from your system tray automatically detecting spam email as it arrives in your Inbox. Using your local black-list, white-list and spam filter plug-ins, as well as publicly available black lists, Spam Monitor will detect and mark spam messages before they reach you.

 

Nevod AntiSpam Key Features:

  • Quick adding of addresses to the white or black list (just copy it to clipboard)
  • Supports connection to unlimited POP3 servers and mailboxes
  • Flexible system of notices on arrival of new messages
  • Global hot keys for addition of a new rule of filtering, reception of new messages, display of a window of the program, start of your post program
  • Creation of backup copies of program settings
